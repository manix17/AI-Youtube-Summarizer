Available Variables:
  • {VIDEO_TITLE} - Inserts the full title of the YouTube video.
  • {CHANNEL_NAME} - Inserts the name of the YouTube channel.
  • {VIDEO_DURATION} - Inserts the duration of the video (e.g., "10:34").
  • {VIDEO_DESCRIPTION} - Inserts the description of the video from the video's details section.
  • {VIDEO_TRANSCRIPT} - Inserts the full, raw transcript of the video.
  • {TARGET_LANGUAGE} - Inserts the language you want the summary to be in.
  • {CURRENT_TIMESTAMP} - Inserts the current date and time when the summary is generated (e.g., "August 8, 2025 at 10:44:58 PM PDT").
  • {VIDEO_DATE} - Inserts the upload date of the video as displayed on YouTube (e.g., "Jan 15, 2024").
